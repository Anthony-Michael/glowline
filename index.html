The doorstep problem

The sale is won or lost at the kitchen table.

Homeowners can't picture $6,000 of lights from a verbal pitch. Today installers juggle a separate mockup app to show the look and a CRM to price and schedule — so the moment that actually closes the deal is split across two tools and rarely happens on the spot. Glowline collapses design, pricing, and the proposal into one screen you can run from the driveway.

How it works

Three taps from photo to signed proposal.

01 — Trace

Draw the roofline

Click along the eaves on a photo of the house. Add a separate run for the garage or porch. Drag any point to fine-tune.

02 — Preview

Light every season

Warm white, Christmas, Halloween, Fourth of July — the roofline glows on their actual house so they can see it before they buy.

03 — Send

Price & propose

Footage becomes a priced, editable estimate. Send a branded proposal link the customer can accept from their phone.

Built for the field

Everything that closes the job. Nothing that doesn't.

On their real house

Upload a phone photo or use the demo home. Set scale from any known length — a garage door, the front door — and every foot is measured and priced.

Permanent or seasonal

Switch pricing models in a tap. Permanent track with a smart controller, or seasonal install-and-takedown — the proposal copy adapts.

Shareable proposals

One link opens a clean, customer-facing proposal with the lit-house preview, totals, and an accept button. Works on any phone.

Print & PDF ready

Every proposal prints to a tidy, branded PDF for the homeowners who still want paper on the fridge.
